WOLFCRAFT'S
GROUND KEEPERS

Learn all about the WolfCraft's Build Team which designs, builds and maintains the server's infrastructure for the entire community to enjoy.

GROUND KEEPER ROLE DESCRIPTIONS

Ground Keeper Leaders are responsible for managing and coordinating the Ground Keepers and their action. They are outside the influence of the Staff Team and are capable of making independant decisions when the team is conflicted. The Ground Keeper Leaders also server as a bridge between Staff and the Ground Keepers for the exchange of progress updates, world updates, and more.
Ground Keepers are official members of the build-team. Following their time as a Ground Keeper-Trainee, they have increased responsibility within the team and on the server now having access to basic CoreProtect commands such as /co inspect which can be used to check shops or bases (only upon request by player or staff member) for theft or griefing. As a Ground Keeper, they can take initiatives on projects with approval from the Ground Keeper Leaders and help maitain or improve existing server infrastructure.
Ground Keeper Trainee's are the newest inductions into the Ground Keeper's. To be inducted, they needed to have demonstrated creative skills and an intent to help the community. During their time as a Ground Keepers Trainee, they will learn how the Ground Keepers work, help on projects and prove their value as a Ground Keepers by participating in projects, suggesting ideas for new projects, etc. These people do not have access to CoreProtect commands.

TEAM DESCRIPTIONS

The Overworld Team is responsible for the designing, building and mantaining of Overworld Server Infrastructures. These include Spwan, Shopping and Gaming Districts, and other communal areas and projects.
The Nether/End Team is responsible for designing, building and mantaining of Nether and End Server Infrastructures. These can inclide the creation of a nether hub, nether highways connecting the four corners of the world, creation of nether roof access points, end portal room, amongst other things.
