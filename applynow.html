APPLYING TO THE
WOLFCRAFT COMMUNITY

Applying and applications for a Minecraft Community can often be a very confusing and scary. Learn more about the WolfCraft application process, what we look for and how to successfully complete it without much trouble.

WHY HAVE AN APPLICATION?


The short answer is; To protect our players and community from player that may wish us harm and ensure that our community is filled with kind, creative and enthusiastic players.

THE APPLICATION PROCESS:


The WolfCraft application process can be submarised five simple steps:

  • Carefully read and understand the community and server rules. You can find them here.
  • Join the WolfCraft discord server.
  • Navigate to the #┊rules channel, read and react to the rules.
  • Fill out the following application (Also found in the #┊how-to-apply channel) and paste it in the #┊application channel:

    • Notice: If you cannot see #┊application , please revisit step 3.

    THE WOLFCRAFT APPLICATION FORM:


    • In-Game-Name:
    • Age:
    • Do you have a mic? (Not a requirement)
    • Country of Origin:
    • Timezone:
    • Describe yourself in 5 words:
    • How long have you been playing Minecraft?
    • How many hours do you tend to play each week?
    • What about WolfCraft stood out to you?
    • Can you tell us a little about yourself? (Occupation, hobbies, favorite games, a random fact about you..)
    • What is your favourite build that you have done in Minecraft? (Please include a photo, if possible.)
    • Anything else you'd like to add?
  • Wait patiently. Once your application is posted in #┊application , a Staff Member will evaluate it and you will be informed via DM's if you were accepted. You may ping specific members of staff in #┊application-support for an update if you havn't recieved an response after a while however do not ping entire staff roles. Remember Staff have lives outside out community and it can sometimes take a couple of hours for you to get a response.

THINGS WE LOOK FOR IN AN APPLICATION


The application is your point of first contact with us and an opportunity for us to meet you. Ironically, you should fill out the application not with what we may be looking for but with what you want us to see and learn about you. We encourage you to be Bold - Creative - Honest. Share everything you think will help us better understand you and why you want to be part of our community. These are key features in most successful applications.
