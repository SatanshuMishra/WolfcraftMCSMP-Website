MEET THE WOLFCRAFT STAFF TEAM.

Every great server needs an even better staff team to guide and protect it. Meet The WolfCraft communities staff team.

STAFF MEMBER ROLE DESCRIPTIONS

Owners are the main administrative power on the server. They overlook the activies of the entire staff team and moniter the server to ensure its running smoothly.
These are the creative, "go-getters" on all fronts to make WolfCraft more fun, enjoyable. They aren't required to do as much of the administration work as the other staff roles however they have the appropriate permissions to do so when needed. Their main job is to develop the server by organising community events, implementing popular community requested changes, and more.
They are the server's police force. They deal with cases involving item duplication, item loss, griefing, hacking, stealing and more. They are the final authority for ban or punishment appeals as well as for determining whether or not players have broken the server rules.
They are members of the staff team who have learnt the basics of console commands and the duties associated with being a staff member. In addition to the tasks they performed as a Helper, they now have access to higher moderation commands.
They are often the newest members of the staff team. They have access to coreprotect and teleport commands to help players in need. They can kick players and mute players, but cannot ban them. They are also the main workforce for checking the shopping district/player bases for theif. They do no hold as many permisions as higher levels of staff.

To learn more about each staff member, click on their individual cards.
