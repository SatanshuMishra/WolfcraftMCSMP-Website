ABOUT OUR COMMUNITY

Learn about the WolfCraft community, Minecraft servers, the features we offer, and more.

THE WOLFCRAFT COMMUNITY


Joining WolfCraft first-and-foremost makes you part of our ever growing community. Our community is filled with enthusiasts who are binded by their love for Minecraft and desire to share their experiences with others or just to have fun along the way.

DISCORD ACCESS

Our official Discord server is the central hub for WolfCraft and all its members. This is a happening place where you can relax, connect with other members, ask questions or even share your work for other to see. Additionally, here you will be able to access gaming channels dedicated to other games outside Minecraft. This is also where you can communicate with staff to help us improve your experience on the server.

DEDICATED STAFF TEAM

When in a multiplayer environment, it is important to have a strong management team. At WolfCraft, you will find a dedicated staff team which actively moniters and protects the server from malicious actions. The staff team is predominantly made up of players who joined the server and have shown dedication to help others, support and maintain the community ensuring they have a player's perspective when making decisions. Learn more about staff roles and the people that manage our community here

INTERNATIONAL & DIVERSE COMMUNITY

The WolfCraft community has over a hundred players thus far and is growing each WolfCraft season. We are proud to also have an international community of players from different backgrounds and countries providing a diverse and unique playing experience.

FEATURES EXPLAINED, PLUGINS & DATAPACKS


Get more information regarding the key-features offered by our Minecraft Servers, and learn what plugins and datapacks we use.

COREPROTECT

To protect player builds from unwanted damage and distruction, we have equiped our servers with the CoreProtect plugin. CoreProtect is a fast, efficient, data logging and anti-griefing tool which allows us to rollback and restore any amount of damage. To learn more about CoreProtects, click here.

DYNAMIC MAP

To help players find the perfect spot for their builds and/or visualise their bases from a different perspective, each season we have a live dynamic map of our survival server showing the locations of builds, players and their movements in real time. To view the current season's map, click here.

PLAYER GENERATED ECONOMY

At WolfCraft, we offer players a functional economy to buy and trade. Each season, players are given the opportunity to build uniquly styled shops and stalls to sell their items in our designated Shopping District. Players can choose to buy goods with the server's standard currency, diamonds, or trade their goods for other goods of value. The value of each item is shaped by its supply-and-demand. However, staff can intervine to ensure the value of certain goods is maintained to ensure new and old players have the same competitive position. To see past examples of shoping districts we have constructed, visit our gallery.

GROUND KEEPERS (COMMUNITY BUILD TEAM)

Each season (i.e. following each reset of the survival server), the Ground Keepers are responsible for designing and constructing key server infrastructures such as Spawn and Shopping District. They are a group of skilled, creative and dedicated players and builders who share an interest towards building something amazing for the community to enjoy. To learn more about our server's elite build team, visit the ground keepers page.
